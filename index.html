Privacy Policy

  1. Introduction
    GratefulTime.app ("the App") prioritizes your privacy and data security. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.
  2. Information We Collect
    1. User Provided Information:
      Upon signing in with Apple, we obtain minimal authentication data. If you consent, we store your email address, full name, and time zone.
    2. Automatic Data:
      No automatic collection of personal data beyond device time zone required for notifications.
  3. Use of Information
    Information is used solely to authenticate, personalize, schedule notifications, generate AI summaries, and improve the app securely.
  4. Data Storage and Security
    We store minimal data securely, without sharing with third parties, adhering to Apple's privacy standards.
  5. AI Summary Generation
    When generating AI summaries, only the text content of gratitude entries is used. User email, full name, and user ID are never included in the AI prompt. No personally identifiable information is transmitted to or processed by the AI model.
  6. User Control and Rights
    You control the information you provide.
    You can update or delete your personal info in settings or by contacting support.
    Opting out of optional data does not affect app use.
    Deleting the app or account removes stored data.
  7. Notifications
    Local notifications are sent at your preferred time and can be disabled in device settings.
  8. Children's Privacy
    The app is not for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.
  9. Changes to This Policy
    We may update this policy; users will be notified of significant changes.
  10. Contact
    Questions? Contact: eszhd@icloud.com
  11. Legal Compliance
    This policy complies with applicable data protection laws, including GDPR and CCPA where relevant.
